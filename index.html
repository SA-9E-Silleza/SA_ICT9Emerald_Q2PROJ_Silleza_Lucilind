Classic Vietnamese Beef Pho

A popular Vietnamese staple, it is a fragrant noodle soup featuring tender beef, silky rice noodles, and a savory broth infused with aromatic spices, served with fresh herbs and lime.

Bánh mì

A Vietnamese sandwich that combines a crispy baguette with savory meats, pickled vegetables, fresh herbs, and a hint of chili and mayonnaise for a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Cao Lầu

A traditional Vietnamese noodle dish from Hội An, featuring thick, chewy noodles, tender pork, fresh herbs, and crispy croutons, all served in a rich, flavorful broth.

Mi Quang

A vibrant Vietnamese noodle dish from Quảng Nam, featuring wide rice noodles, flavorful broth, shrimp or pork, fresh herbs, and crunchy peanuts, often topped with a fried rice cracker.

Bún Thang

A Hanoi-style Vietnamese noodle soup made with delicate rice noodles, shredded chicken, pork, egg, and herbs, served in a clear, savory broth for a light yet flavorful dish.

Rau muống

Also known as water spinach, is a popular Vietnamese vegetable, often stir-fried with garlic or cooked in soups, known for its crisp texture and mild, slightly peppery flavor.
