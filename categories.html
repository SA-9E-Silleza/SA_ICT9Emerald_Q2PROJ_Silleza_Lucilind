Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Peppercorns

Steps:

  1. Marinate meat in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic for 30 minutes.
  2. Sauté garlic, then add marinated meat and bay leaves.
  3. Simmer until meat is tender and sauce thickens.
Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork (ribs or belly)
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 packet tamarind soup mix
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • Vegetables (eggplant, radish, string beans, spinach)

Steps:

  1. Boil pork with onion and tomatoes until tender.
  2. Add tamarind mix and vegetables. Simmer until cooked.
  3. Season with fish sauce or salt to taste. Serve hot.
Lechon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole pig (or pork belly for Lechon Roll)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic, onion, and lemongrass (for stuffing)

Steps:

  1. Clean and season the pig inside and out with salt and pepper.
  2. Stuff with garlic, onion, and lemongrass.
  3. Roast over charcoal until the skin is crispy. Serve with liver sauce.
Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg oxtail or beef tripe
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 cups water
  • Vegetables (eggplant, banana heart, string beans, pechay)
  • 1/4 cup annatto seeds (for coloring)
  • Ground rice or cornstarch (thickener)

Steps:

  1. Boil oxtail until tender.
  2. Add peanut butter and annatto water for coloring.
  3. Add vegetables and simmer until cooked. Serve with bagoong (shrimp paste).
Halo-Halo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • Various fruits (banana, jackfruit, etc.)
  • Leche flan
  • Ube halaya
  • Scoops of ice cream

Steps:

  1. Layer fruits and toppings in a tall glass.
  2. Add shaved ice and pour milk over it.
  3. Top with ice cream and serve immediately.
Pancit Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g bihon (rice noodles)
  • 1 cup chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup assorted vegetables (carrots, cabbage, celery)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced

Steps:

  1. Sauté garlic and onion. Add chicken and cook until done.
  2. Add vegetables and soy sauce. Stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  3. Soak bihon in water, then drain and add to the pan.
  4. Mix well and cook until heated through. Serve hot.
