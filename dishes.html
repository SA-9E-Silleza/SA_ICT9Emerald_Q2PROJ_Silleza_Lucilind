Pho Bo Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Beef broth (preferably simmered for several hours)
  • Rice noodles (Bánh phở)
  • Thinly sliced beef (sirloin or brisket)
  • Herbs like cilantro, Thai basil, and mint
  • Bean sprouts
  • Lime wedges
  • Chili peppers
  • Fish sauce, hoisin sauce, and sriracha sauce
Instructions:

1. Cook the rice noodles then you may also drain and set aside.

2. In a large pot (atleast around 6L), bring the beef broth to a boil, and then reduce to a simmer for a minute or so.

3. Add the thinly sliced beef to the hot broth for a few seconds to cook it.

4. Place the cooked noodles in a bowl, pour the hot broth and beef over the noodles.

5. Garnish with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime, and chili peppers. Serve with fish sauce, hoisin, and sriracha on the side.

Banh Mi Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Baguette (light and crispy)
  • Char siu pork or grilled chicken
  • Pickled carrots and daikon
  • Cucumber slices
  • Fresh cilantro and mint
  • Chili peppers (optional)
  • Mayonnaise or pate
Instructions:

1. Slice the baguette in half and spread a layer of mayonnaise or pate on each side.

2. Layer the cooked pork or grilled chicken inside the baguette.

3. Add pickled carrots, daikon, cucumber, fresh cilantro, mint, and chili peppers (if using).

4. Close the sandwich and enjoy! It's a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Mi Quang Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Yellow rice noodles (Mi Quang noodles)
  • Shrimp and pork (or chicken)
  • Herbs (Vietnamese mint, cilantro)
  • Roasted peanuts
  • Rice crackers (bánh tráng)
  • Fish sauce, lime, and chili for seasoning
Instructions:

1. Boil the noodles and drain them.

2. Stir-fry shrimp, pork, or chicken with garlic and shallots until cooked.

3. Add a little broth, and then mix the noodles into the stir-fried meat and herbs.

4. Serve with roasted peanuts, rice crackers, and lime on the side. Enjoy!

Bun Thang Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Thin rice noodles
  • Chicken breast, shredded
  • Egg, boiled and sliced
  • Vietnamese pork (Giò lụa)
  • Fish sauce and lime
  • Herbs (Vietnamese cilantro, green onions)
Instructions:

1. Cook the rice noodles according to the instructions.

2. Prepare the shredded chicken and slice the pork and boiled eggs.

3. Boil the broth with fish sauce and lime for a light, flavorful base.

4. Combine the noodles, chicken, pork, and egg in a bowl, then pour the hot broth over it.

5. Garnish with fresh herbs and enjoy the light yet savory soup!

Rau Muong Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Water spinach (Rau muống)
  • Garlic, minced
  • Fish sauce and soy sauce
  • Chili peppers (optional)
Instructions:

1. Wash the water spinach thoroughly and cut into manageable pieces.

2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté minced garlic until golden.

3. Add the water spinach and stir-fry for a few minutes, seasoning with fish sauce and soy sauce.

4. Garnish with chili peppers if desired, and serve as a side dish to any meal.
